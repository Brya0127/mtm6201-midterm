It was time for A Frest Tart

After Trudy was widowed and her whole life turned upside down, she decided it was time for a fresh start. Trudy had always loved baking, a skill that she learned at her grandfather's side from the time she was old enough to climb a stoll and wield a whisk. Her wife had always suggested she open a bakery, but Trudy considered it a bit of a pipe dream. But after recovering from her loss, she decided life is too short and unpredictable to make choices that don't fullfill you. With that epiphany in mind, it became her mission to open a bakery and pursue her passion.
